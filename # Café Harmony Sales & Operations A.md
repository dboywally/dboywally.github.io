# **# Café Harmony Sales \& Operations Analysis**



### **##  Project Overview**



Café Harmony is a multi-location café business focused on optimizing sales performance, improving inventory management, and enhancing customer satisfaction. This project leverages Microsoft Excel to analyze menu performance, branch performance, stock levels, and customer feedback to uncover business insights and provide actionable recommendations.



\---



### **##  Business Problem**



Café Harmony faced several operational challenges:



\- Identifying top-performing and underperforming menu items.

\- Measuring sales performance across different locations.

\- Managing inventory shortages and excess stock.

\- Understanding customer satisfaction levels.

\- Improving overall operational efficiency and profitability.

### **#  Dashboard Analysis**



\---



### **# 1. Menu Performance Analysis**



\### Business Question



Which products generate the most revenue, and how can menu performance be optimized?



#### **### Key Insights**



**#### • Balanced Revenue Distribution**

Revenue is relatively balanced across products, indicating a diversified menu with no overreliance on a single item.



**#### • Best-Selling Products**

Muffins, Cappuccinos, and Iced Tea generated the highest revenues, showing strong customer preference.



**#### • Underperforming Products**

Sandwiches and Croissants recorded comparatively lower revenue and may require additional attention.



**#### • Beverage Categories Drive Sales**

Drinks contribute more revenue collectively than food items, suggesting stronger demand and higher revenue potential.



### **### Recommendations**



\- Promote low-performing items through bundles and discounts.

\- Increase visibility of top-selling products.

\- Expand beverage offerings with seasonal variations.

\- Introduce cross-selling opportunities such as "Coffee + Muffin" combos.

\- Gather customer feedback on low-performing menu items.



\---



### **# 2. Branch Performance Analysis**



\### Business Question



How are the various locations performing against their sales targets?



#### **### Key Insights**



**#### • Downtown is the Strongest Branch**

Downtown consistently generated the highest sales and exceeded targets.



**#### • Airport Branch Performs Well**

Airport maintains healthy sales and performs above target.



**#### • Suburbs Show Growth Potential**

Sales performance remains competitive with opportunities for improvement.



**#### • Uptown is the Weakest Branch**

Uptown lags behind the other locations in overall performance.



**#### • Revenue Contribution is Uneven**

Business growth relies heavily on Downtown, increasing concentration risk.



### **### Recommendations**



\- Continue investing in Downtown operations.

\- Investigate factors affecting Uptown sales.

\- Introduce localized promotions at Airport and Suburbs locations.

\- Diversify growth efforts across all branches.

\- Analyze customer purchasing behavior to identify opportunities.



\---



### **# 3. Inventory Management Analysis**



\### Business Question



How effectively is inventory being managed, and where are shortages or surpluses occurring?



#### **### Key Insights**



\#### • Critical Stock Shortages Exist

Milk, Croissant Dough, Muffin Mix, and Iced Tea Bags are frequently understocked, risking lost sales.



\#### • Overstocking Ties Up Capital

Bread, Sugar, and Coffee Beans have excess inventory, increasing storage and wastage costs.



\#### • Demand and Supply are Misaligned

High-demand ingredients are understocked while slower-moving items are overstocked.



\#### • Inventory Balance Varies by Product

Some products are maintained effectively while others require better planning.



\#### • Procurement Processes Need Improvement

Current replenishment strategies are not fully aligned with customer demand.



### **### Recommendations**



\- Prioritize replenishment of critical inventory items.

\- Reduce purchase quantities for surplus products.

\- Implement demand forecasting techniques.

\- Introduce low-stock alert systems.

\- Work with suppliers to reduce lead times.

\- Apply ABC inventory classification to optimize stock control.



\---



### **# 4. Customer Feedback Analysis**



\### Business Question



What does customer feedback reveal about overall customer satisfaction?



#### **### Key Insights**



\#### • Customer Dissatisfaction is High

Seventy percent of customers expressed dissatisfaction, indicating significant service gaps.



\#### • Positive Perception Still Exists

Thirty percent of customers rated the experience positively, demonstrating underlying value.



\#### • Issues are More Execution-Related

Customer concerns appear to be driven more by service delivery than by the products themselves.



**#### • Customer Loyalty is at Risk**

Continued dissatisfaction may reduce repeat business and damage brand reputation.



**#### • Improvement Opportunities Exist**

Customer sentiment can improve with targeted actions and continuous feedback.



### **### Recommendations**



\- Conduct deeper customer feedback analysis.

\- Identify and address key pain points.

\- Leverage positive feedback in marketing campaigns.

\- Communicate improvements to customers.

\- Continuously monitor customer satisfaction levels.
