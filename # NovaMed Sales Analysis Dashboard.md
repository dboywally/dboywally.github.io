# **# NovaMed Sales Analysis Dashboard**



### **## Project Overview**



NovaMed Solutions is a pharmaceutical distributor serving healthcare providers across multiple regions. The company sought to evaluate sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and market opportunities to improve strategic decision-making.



This project analyzes sales and profitability trends, customer segments, and product performance to uncover actionable insights that support growth and operational efficiency.



\---



### **## Business Objectives**



The analysis was designed to answer the following questions:



\- How has revenue and profit performed over time?

\- Which products contribute the most to profitability?

\- Which customer groups generate the highest revenue?

\- Are there risks associated with product concentration?

\- How can NovaMed improve customer engagement and market expansion?

### **## Dashboard Analysis**



#### **### Revenue and Profit Performance**



\- Revenue remained stable throughout the year, averaging approximately \*\*$5.9M monthly\*\*.

\- September recorded the highest revenue at \*\*$6.5M\*\*.

\- February and June experienced slight dips.

\- Profit closely followed revenue trends, indicating healthy operational efficiency.



#### **### Product Performance**



#### **#### Top Profit-Generating Products**



\- Doxycycline

\- Ergocalciferol

\- Lisinopril

\- Simvastatin

\- Clonazepam



\#### Lowest Performing Products



\- Montelukast

\- Hydrochlorothiazide

\- Warfarin

\- Fluticasone

\- Amoxicillin



#### **### Customer Insights**



#### **#### Age Group Analysis**



\- Middle-aged customers generated the highest revenue.

\- Adult and old-age customers contributed significantly.

\- Youth and young adults represent growth opportunities.



#### **#### Gender Analysis**



\- Male customers generated the highest revenue.

\- Female and other customer groups present additional growth potential.



#### **#### Buyer Type Analysis**



\- Users accounted for approximately \*\*88%\*\* of revenue.

\- Sellers generated significantly lower revenue despite equal representation.



\#### Geographic Distribution



Revenue was distributed across:



\- North America

\- Europe

\- Asia

\- Australia



This indicates a diversified global customer base.



\---



### **##  Key Insights**



#### **### 1. Exceptional Profitability**



NovaMed achieved a profit margin of nearly \*\*82%\*\*, demonstrating strong pricing power and efficient cost management.



#### **### 2. Stable Revenue Stream**



Sales remained consistent throughout the year, reducing operational risk.



#### **### 3. Diversified Customer Base**



No single customer contributes significantly to total sales, minimizing dependency risk.



#### **### 4. Product Concentration Risk**



A few products—particularly Doxycycline, Ergocalciferol, and Lisinopril—generate a large portion of profits.



#### **### 5. Untapped Market Opportunities**



Youth demographics and seller channels remain underutilized growth segments.



\---



### **## 💡 Recommendations**



#### **### Expand Younger Customer Segments**



Launch digital health campaigns, subscription plans, and promotional bundles targeted at younger consumers.



#### **### Strengthen Customer Loyalty**



Introduce rewards programs and personalized healthcare offerings for adult and middle-aged customers.



#### **### Improve Seller Engagement**



Provide reseller incentives, wholesale discounts, and partnership programs to increase seller contributions.



#### **### Optimize Low-Performing Products**



Review pricing strategies and bundle low-margin products with top-performing products.



#### **### Diversify Product Portfolio**



Reduce dependency on a few products by expanding product offerings and developing new revenue streams.



#### **### Geographic Expansion**



Increase market penetration in Asia and emerging healthcare markets through strategic partnerships.



\---



### **##  Conclusion**



NovaMed Solutions demonstrates strong financial performance, exceptional profitability, and a diversified customer base. However, future growth depends on reducing product concentration risk, improving customer retention, and expanding into underserved market segments.



The insights generated from this dashboard provide a foundation for data-driven decision-making and long-term sustainable growth.
