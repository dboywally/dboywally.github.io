BrightCart Website Traffic Analysis

## Project Overview

BrightCart faced limited visibility into website performance, making it difficult to understand customer behavior, identify high-performing traffic sources, and optimize conversions. This project analyzes website traffic patterns and provides actionable recommendations to improve marketing effectiveness and business performance.

---

## Business Challenges

* Traffic timing uncertainty.
* Difficulty identifying the most effective traffic sources.
* Conversion optimization opportunities not fully utilized.
* Limited customer behavior insights.
* Limited performance reporting.

---

## Project Objectives

The project aimed to:

* Analyze website traffic timing patterns.
* Evaluate traffic source effectiveness.
* Identify opportunities to improve conversion rates.
* Understand customer behavior across multiple locations.
* Provide interactive reporting to support decision-making.

---

# Data Cleaning Process

Before analysis and visualization, the dataset was cleaned and transformed to ensure consistency and accuracy.

### Data Quality Assessment

* Reviewed the dataset for missing values and inconsistencies.
* Verified column formats and data types.

### Duplicate Removal

* Removed duplicate records to avoid overcounting users and conversions.

### Standardization of Categorical Variables

Standardized inconsistent values across:

* Gender
* Countries
* Traffic Source categories

### Handling Missing Values

* Identified and addressed incomplete records.

### Date and Time Formatting

* Converted date and time fields into appropriate formats.
* Created time-based categories for hourly analysis.

### Derived Fields Created

* Conversion Flag
* Hour of Visit
* Conversion Metrics
* Traffic Source Categories

### Validation and Quality Checks

* Verified KPI totals.
* Cross-checked values with dashboard outputs.

### Data Transformation for Reporting

* Structured the data for Power BI reporting and visualization.

# Key Insights

## Insight 1: Morning and Evening Hours Drive Higher Conversion Rates

### Evidence

The Conversion Rate by Hour analysis revealed conversion peaks during:

* 9 AM – 11 AM
* 8 PM – 10 PM

### Business Impact

Customers demonstrate stronger purchase intent during these periods.

---

## Insight 2: Organic Search Generates the Highest Number of Conversions

### Evidence

Organic Search outperformed other traffic sources.

### Business Impact

SEO-driven visitors exhibit stronger engagement and conversion behavior.

---

## Insight 3: Direct Traffic Generates High Sessions but Lower Conversions

### Evidence

Direct Traffic produced the highest volume of sessions, while Organic Search generated more conversions.

### Business Impact

Many visitors are not completing desired actions, indicating opportunities for funnel optimization.

---

## Insight 4: Paid Ads and Social Media Deliver Lower Conversion Performance

### Evidence

Paid Ads and Social Media channels contributed fewer conversions.

### Business Impact

Marketing resources may not be producing optimal returns.

---

## Insight 5: Website Traffic Is Geographically Diverse

### Evidence

The dashboard map showed traffic from multiple countries.

### Business Impact

Regional differences create opportunities for targeted marketing campaigns.

---

# Recommendations

## Recommendation 1

Schedule:

* Promotional campaigns
* Email marketing
* Paid advertising

during peak conversion windows to maximize ROI.

---

## Recommendation 2

Increase investment in SEO through:

* Keyword optimization
* Blog content
* Technical website improvements

to attract more high-converting visitors.

---

## Recommendation 3

Improve conversion paths for direct visitors by:

* Simplifying landing pages
* Strengthening calls-to-action
* Improving user experience and checkout processes

---

## Recommendation 4

Review:

* Ad targeting
* Audience segmentation
* Ad creatives
* Campaign messaging

and allocate resources toward better-performing channels.

---

## Recommendation 5

Develop region-specific campaigns using:

* Localized promotions
* Personalized offers
* Country-specific messaging

to improve engagement and conversion rates.

---

# Executive Summary

* Organic Search is the strongest-performing acquisition channel.
* Peak conversion windows should guide campaign timing.
* Direct Traffic offers untapped conversion opportunities.
* Underperforming channels require optimization.
* Geographic segmentation can improve marketing effectiveness.
