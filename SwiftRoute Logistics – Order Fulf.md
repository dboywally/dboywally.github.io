# **# SwiftRoute Logistics – Order Fulfilment Optimization**



### **## Project Overview**



SwiftRoute Logistics Ltd. is a UK-based logistics company serving retailers, manufacturers, and e-commerce businesses. As order volumes increased, the company faced operational inefficiencies, inconsistent delivery times, and rising customer complaints.



This project involved analyzing operational data and developing an interactive Power BI dashboard to identify operational bottlenecks, monitor delivery performance, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve order fulfilment efficiency.



\---



### **## Business Problem**



SwiftRoute Logistics experienced several operational challenges:



\- Growing order backlogs.

\- Inconsistent delivery times.

\- Limited visibility into order progress.

\- Increasing customer dissatisfaction.

\- Lack of centralized reporting and performance tracking.



Without a structured reporting solution, management struggled to identify root causes of delays and make proactive operational decisions.



\---



### **## Project Objectives**



The objectives of this project were to:



\- Analyze delivery performance and customer feedback.

\- Identify operational bottlenecks and backlog drivers.

\- Evaluate route efficiency and allocation strategies.

\- Measure driver performance across delivery routes.

\- Provide actionable recommendations to improve customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

### **# Key Insights**



### **## 1. Delivery Delays Significantly Impact Customer Satisfaction**



\### Observation



Customer sentiment declines sharply once delivery delays exceed 10 minutes. Delays beyond 20 minutes result in significantly lower satisfaction levels.



\### Business Impact



Delivery delays directly affect customer trust and overall customer experience.



\---



#### **## 2. Backlogs Are Concentrated in Specific Zones**



\### Observation



Zone 1 recorded the highest backlog levels, while Zone 3 demonstrated the strongest operational performance.



\### Business Impact



Backlog challenges are concentrated in specific geographic areas rather than across the entire operation.



\---



#### **## 3. Standard Allocation Rules Outperform Expedited Rules**



\### Observation



Orders processed using Standard Allocation Rules experienced lower average delays compared to Expedited Allocation Rules.



\### Business Impact



Additional process complexity appears to reduce operational efficiency.



\---



#### **## 4. Route 3 Represents a Major Operational Bottleneck**



\### Observation



Route 3 recorded the highest average delivery delays among all routes.



\### Business Impact



Route design and planning significantly influence delivery performance.



\---



#### **## 5. Significant Driver Performance Variability Exists**



\### Observation



Top-performing drivers completed deliveries approximately 20–30 minutes faster than lower-performing drivers.



\### Business Impact



Inconsistent driver performance contributes to unreliable service levels.



\---



### **# Recommendations**



#### **## Recommendation 1: Make Standard Allocation Rules the Default Process**



\### Actions



\- Use Standard Allocation Rules for most deliveries.

\- Reserve Expedited Rules for urgent deliveries only.



\### Expected Impact



\- Reduced average delays.

\- Improved delivery consistency.

\- Lower operational complexity.



\---



#### **## Recommendation 2: Address Zone 1 Backlogs**



\### Actions



\- Increase temporary delivery capacity in Zone 1.

\- Reallocate resources from lower-volume zones.

\- Conduct a backlog reduction initiative.



\### Expected Impact



\- Lower backlog rates.

\- Faster order fulfilment.

\- Improved customer satisfaction.



\---



#### **## Recommendation 3: Optimize Route 3**



\### Actions



\- Review route structure and traffic conditions.

\- Split Route 3 into smaller service areas.

\- Assign experienced drivers to complex routes.



\### Expected Impact



\- Reduced delays.

\- Improved route efficiency.

\- More consistent delivery performance.



\---



#### **## Recommendation 4: Improve Driver Performance**



\### Actions



\- Pair high-performing drivers with lower-performing drivers.

\- Standardize operating procedures.

\- Align route complexity with driver capability.



\### Expected Impact



\- Reduced performance variability.

\- Increased service reliability.

\- Faster deliveries.



\---



#### **## Recommendation 5: Protect Customer Satisfaction**



\### Actions



\- Trigger customer notifications for delays exceeding 10 minutes.

\- Provide proactive communication and recovery measures.

\- Implement customer service follow-up procedures.



\### Expected Impact



\- Improved customer trust.

\- Reduced complaints.

\- Higher customer retention.



\---



\# Business Impact



By implementing these recommendations, SwiftRoute Logistics can:



\- Reduce delivery delays.

\- Improve customer satisfaction.

\- Lower backlog rates.

\- Increase operational efficiency.

\- Improve service reliability.

\- Enable proactive decision-making through data-driven reporting.
